How To Submit Manuscripts

Manuscripts must be formatted in MS Word (12 point, 1’’ in ALL). The manuscripts should not exceed 15 pages unless the authors are willing to pay extra charge at the time of publication. Submitted papers should not have been published or under consideration in another journal.

The Editorial Board reserves the right not to consider papers that exceed the approved length or have been submitted or published elsewhere.

Submissions must follow structure: Introduction, methods, analysis, results, conclusions, and References (APA-6 Edition) or another international referencing Style.

Submit online via Email @ benacademicpool@aol.com, and beneapoolpub@gmail.com.
